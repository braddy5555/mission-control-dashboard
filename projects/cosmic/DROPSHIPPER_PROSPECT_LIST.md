## Lead Scoring

### Criteria (1-10 scale):
1. **Ad Spend** (Hoe hoger, hoe warmer de lead)
2. **Product Type** (Consumables > One-time purchases)
3. **Community Engagement** (Actief op social = warmer)
4. **Tech Openness** (Al geïnteresseerd in innovatie)
5. **Pain Urgency** (Actief zoekend naar oplossingen)

**Score 40+ = Hot Lead (prioriteit 1)**
**Score 30-39 = Warm Lead (prioriteit 2)**
**Score <30 = Cold Lead (nurture sequence)**

---

## Follow-up Sequence

### Day 1: Initial Outreach
- Cold email OR LinkedIn DM
- Attach lead magnet PDF

### Day 3: Value Add
- Share relevant case study
- No pitch, pure value

### Day 5: Soft CTA
- "Saw this and thought of you"
- Link to strategy call booking

### Day 7: Social Proof
- Share testimonial from similar niche
- Direct booking link

### Day 10: Final Attempt
- Breakup email
- "Probably not a fit right now, but keeping you on the radar"
